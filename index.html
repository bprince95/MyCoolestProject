Air quality vs. California Earthquakes

We believe that earthquake events will cause an increase in carbon monoxide, sulfur dioxide, nitrogen dioxide and ozone in the area radiating from the earthquake epicenter.

This hyptohesis is based on a Taiwanese study that found a slight correlation. Due to it's propensity for having earthquakes we wanted to see if this would apply to California.

    Our conclusions:
    1. There is no correlation between earthquakes and the release of noxious gases from the earth.
